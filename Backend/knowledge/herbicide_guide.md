## CROP-SPECIFIC WEED MANAGEMENT

### Wheat
**Key weeds:** Phalaris minor (Canary grass), Wild oat (Avena sp.)

| Product | Active Ingredient | Dose | Weeds Controlled |
|---|---|---|---|
| MAACHIS | Clodinafop-propargyl 15% WP | 400 gm/ha | Phalaris minor only |
| PATINA | Pinoxaden 5.1% EC | 800-900 ml/ha | Phalaris minor + Wild oat |

**Decision guide:**
- **MAACHIS** if only Phalaris minor (Canary grass) is the problem
- **PATINA** if both Phalaris minor AND Wild oat (Avena sp.) are present; also has better rain fastness (60 min)
- Both have wide window of application and superior crop tolerance

### Rice (Transplanted)
**Key weeds:** Echinochloa spp., Cyperus spp., Digitaria sanguinalis

| Product | Active Ingredient | Dose | Timing |
|---|---|---|---|
| RIMOVE PLUS | Pretilachlor 37% EW | 1500-1875 ml/ha | Pre-emergence |

**Notes:** Apply within 3-5 days of transplanting; can be used on any rice variety; no phytotoxicity on paddy

### Cotton
**Key weeds:** Trianthema spp., Amaranthus, Digitaria, Celosia

| Product | Active Ingredient | Dose | Weeds | Timing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| NANCHAKU | Pyrithiobac Sodium 10% EC | 625-750 ml/ha | Broad-leaf only | Post-emergence |
| NANCHAKU POWER | Pyrithiobac 6% + Quizalofop 4% MEC | 1000-1250 ml/ha | Broad-leaf + Grasses | Pre + early post |
| HITBAC | Pendimethalin 38.7% CS | 1500-1700 ml/ha | Annual grasses, sedges, broadleaf | Pre-emergence |

**Decision guide:**
- **NANCHAKU:** When only broad-leaf weeds are present
- **NANCHAKU POWER:** One-shot solution for both grass and broad-leaf weeds; no tank-mixing needed
- **HITBAC:** Use as pre-emergence to prevent weed germination

### Tea & Plantation Crops
| Product | Active Ingredient | Dose | Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| BRAKE | Glyphosate 41% SL | 2000-3000 ml/ha | Non-selective systemic |
| BRAKE G | Glyphosate 71% SG | 3000 gm/ha | Non-selective systemic |
| PARQ | Paraquat 24% SL | 2000-3000 ml/ha | Non-selective contact |

**Difference between BRAKE and PARQ:**
- **BRAKE (Glyphosate):** Systemic; absorbed by foliage and translocated to roots; kills from roots up; takes 7-12 days; effective on perennials
- **PARQ (Paraquat):** Contact only; fast knockdown of green tissue; biologically inactive in soil; annual weeds

## PRODUCT PROFILES

### HITBAC (Pendimethalin 38.7% CS)
- Forms a herbicide barrier at soil surface preventing weed germination
- Residual control kills weeds as they emerge
- **No residual effect on subsequent/follow crop** — safe crop rotation
- Crops: Soybean, Cotton, Onion, Chilli, Groundnut, Mustard, Cumin

### MAACHIS (Clodinafop-propargyl 15% WP)
- Inhibits ACCase enzyme in grass weeds
- Active growth ceases within **48 hours** of application
- Wide window of application — flexibility for time of application
- Gives control even under unfavorable climatic conditions

### PATINA (Pinoxaden 5.1% EC)
- Quickly translocates within plant acropetally & basipetally
- Good rain fastness — **60 minutes**
- Weed growth stops immediately
- Superior control across broad spectrum of grass weeds
- Safe to Wheat crop and subsequent crops

### NANCHAKU POWER (Pyrithiobac 6% + Quizalofop 4% MEC)
- ME (Micro-Emulsion) formulation — thermodynamically stable; improves bioavailability
- Controls BOTH narrow and broad leaf weeds in ONE application
- No tank mixing required
- Safe to cotton at any stage of growth

### DESTAL XTRA (Mesotrione 2.27% + Atrazine 22.7% SC)
- Unique HPPD + PS II chemistry
- In-built adjuvant — no separate mixing needed
- Ready-mix combination for better weed control than single molecules

### BRAKE (Glyphosate 41% SL)
- Non-selective systemic — translocates through xylem to roots and rhizomes
- Kills perennial weeds from roots
- Does not affect germination of ensuing crops
- Any crop can be grown after its application

### REMEDUO (Fomesafen + Fluazifop-P-Butyl)
- Fomesafen: PPO inhibitor — controls broad-leaf weeds
- Fluazifop-P-Butyl: ACCase inhibitor — controls grass weeds
- Combined: Controls BOTH grass and broad-leaf weeds in one application
- Fast acting; no residual effect

## HERBICIDE SAFETY PRECAUTIONS

1. **BRAKE / PARQ (non-selective):** Never allow spray to drift onto crop leaves — will kill the crop
2. **DESTAL XTRA (Atrazine-based):** Observe soil persistence; avoid in rotation with sensitive crops
3. **RIMOVE PLUS:** Apply within recommended window after transplanting; do not apply during active growth stages
4. **Add 4 IN 1 Sticker** to herbicide sprays for better adhesion and rain fastness
5. Always spray when weeds are young and actively growing for best results
6. Calibrate sprayer accurately — over-dosing may damage crop, under-dosing may fail to control weeds
