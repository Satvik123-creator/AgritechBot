# Biostadt Pest Management Guide

Comprehensive reference for managing insect pests using Biostadt insecticides.

---

## INSECTICIDE CATEGORIES BY MODE OF ACTION

### Neonicotinoids (Systemic — sucking pests)
- **ULTIMO** (Imidacloprid 17.8% SL) — High selectivity; seed treatment option
- **ULTIMO SUPER** (Imidaclopride 30.5% SC) — Enhanced SC formulation; phytotonic effect
- **EDAN** (Imidacloprid 70% WG) — Phytotonic effect; vigorous plant growth
- **EVIDENT** (Thiamethoxam 25% WG) — Granular; soil drench option; paddy nursery
- **EVIDENT FLO** (Thiamethoxam 30% FS) — Seed treatment formulation
- **WAPKIL** (Acetamiprid 20% SP) — Triple action: ovicidal, adulticidal, larvicidal

### Pyrethroids (Contact & Stomach — broad spectrum)
- **AUZAR** (Cypermethrin 25% EC) — Lepidopteran pests; longer persistence
- **KLINTOP** (Bifenthrin 10% EC) — Phyto-tonic effect; mites + insects
- **STOP** (Alphamethrin 10% EC) — Quick knockdown; ovicidal action

### Phenylpyrazoles / Fipronil (Soil & Foliar — broad spectrum)
- **JAANBAZ SC** (Fipronil 5% SC) — Contact + stomach + systemic
- **JAANBAZ GR** (Fipronil 0.3% GR) — Soil application; termites, borers
- **JAANBAZ POWER** (Thiamethoxam 0.9% + Fipronil 0.2% WG) — Dual nervous system action

### Diamides (Lepidopteran specialists)
- **TREMOR** (Chlorantraniliprole 18.5% SC) — Fast cessation of feeding; long duration; IPM safe
- **FULSTOP-D** (Flubendiamide 20% WG) — All stages of caterpillars; RRM technology

### Avermectins (Trans-laminar — Lepidoptera + mites)
- **BIOCLAIM** (Emamectin Benzoate 5% SG) — Naturally occurring; trans-laminar
- **BIOCLAIM PRO** (Emamectin Benzoate 1.5% + Profenophos 35% WDG) — Dual mode; 24-48 hr kill

### Acaricides (Mite specialists)
- **MAIDEN** (Hexythiazox 5.45% EC) — First IGR Acaricide in India; ovicidal + larvicidal + nymphicidal; no phytotoxicity

### Others
- **DERBY** (Diafenthiuron 50% WP) — Vapor action; sucking complex + mites; phytotonic
- **DARTRIZ SP** (Cartap Hydrochloride 50% SP) — Controls all insect stages
- **PRAHAR-C** (Profenophos 40% EC + Cypermethrin 4% EC) — Organophosphorus + pyrethroid
- **KRUSH** (Quinolphos 25% EC) — Broad spectrum; contact + stomach
- **ULKA 505** (Chlorpyriphos 50% + Cypermethrin 5% EC) — Broad spectrum; rain resistant
- **STRIKE** (Dinotefuran 15% + Pymetrozine 45% WG) — BPH specialist; SPB technology

## PEST-SPECIFIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### Brown Plant Hopper (BPH) / Rice Hoppers

- **STRIKE** at 33 gm/ha — Makes BPH IMMOBILE; stops feeding and egg laying
- **EVIDENT** at 100 gm/ha
- **ULTIMO** at 100-125 ml/ha
- **WAPKIL** at 50-100 gm/ha

## IPM PRODUCT SELECTION GUIDE

| Pest Type | IPM-Preferred Products | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Caterpillars/Borers | TREMOR, FULSTOP-D, BIOCLAIM | Safer to beneficial insects |
| Sucking pests | EVIDENT, ULTIMO, WAPKIL | Selective; low impact on natural enemies |
| Mites | MAIDEN | No adverse effect on beneficial insects; no cross resistance |
| Soil pests | JAANBAZ GR, JAANBAZ POWER | Targeted soil application |
| General protection | HITONE (Immunity Booster) | Zero PHI; zero crop residue |

## RESISTANCE MANAGEMENT TIPS
1. Rotate between different classes: Neonicotinoids → Pyrethroids → Diamides → Avermectins
2. TREMOR and FULSTOP-D: no cross resistance; preserve efficacy by rotation
3. MAIDEN: no cross resistance to mites tolerant to conventional acaricides
4. STRIKE uses unique SPB technology — no known resistance in BPH
5. Combine chemical control with BIOZYME biostimulants to build plant vigor and natural resistance
