# Biostadt Disease Management Guide

This guide provides a comprehensive reference for managing crop diseases using Biostadt fungicides and related products.

## FUNGICIDE CATEGORIES

### Systemic Fungicides (absorbed and translocated within plant)
- **ROKO** (Thiophanate Methyl 70% WP) — Preventive + Curative + Systemic; wide label claims
- **ROKO FLO** (Thiophanate Methyl 41.7% SC) — Liquid version; no dust hazard
- **BIOMYCIN** (Kasugamycin 3% SL) — Antibiotic; fungicidal + bactericidal; no resistance to Paddy blast
- **ALLIANCE** (Azoxystrobin 11% + Tebuconazole 18.3% SC) — Dual mode; excellent rain fastness
- **DECIMER** (Azoxystrobin 18.2% + Difenconazole 11.4% SC) — Phytotonic effect
- **CYGNET** (Myclobutanil 10% WP) — Xylem mobile; vapor activity; 1 hr rainfastness
- **REDEEM** (Difenconazole 25% EC) — Rapid absorption; cost effective
- **TAGMA** (Tebuconazole 25.9% EC) — Prophylactic + Curative + Eradicative
- **TRIGGER PRO** (Hexaconazole 5% SC) — Protective, curative and antisporulant
- **SUPERMATE** (Zineb 68% + Hexaconazole 4% WP) — Zinc nutrition bonus; greening effect
- **RINZEX** (Tebuconazole 6.7% + Captan 26.9% SC) — Acts on spore germination, haustoria, hyphal growth

### Contact Fungicides (protective; remain on leaf surface)
- **BENDACO** (Carbendazim 12% + Mancozeb 63% WP) — Systemic + Contact; classic proven
- **SPARSH** (Mancozeb 75% WP) — Broad spectrum; provides Zn & Mn; frost protection
- **DOLBY** (Propineb 70% WP) — Multi site; zinc nutrition; no resistance
- **CAPTEK** (Captan 50% WP) — Effective from seedling to maturity; seed borne and soil borne diseases
- **DUOGUARD** (Cymoxanil 8% + Mancozeb 64% WP) — Rapid penetration (3 hr); against oomycetes
- **SANCHAR** (Metalaxyl 8% + Mancozeb 64% WP) — Downy mildew specialist
- **WOKOVIT** (Sulphur 80% WG) — Triple action: fungicide + Sulphur nutrient + miticide

## DISEASE-SPECIFIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### Powdery Mildew
**Best options:** CYGNET (Myclobutanil), ALLIANCE, TRIGGER PRO, WOKOVIT (Sulphur)
- **Grapes:** CYGNET at 40 gm/10 lit; TRIGGER PRO at 500-1000 ml/ha; WOKOVIT at 1875-2500 gm/ha
- **Apple:** ALLIANCE at 1 ml/lit; RINZEX at 2.5 ml/lit; SUPERMATE at 25 gm/10 lit
- **Wheat:** WOKOVIT at 2500 gm/ha
- **Chilli:** CYGNET at 40 gm/10 lit; RINZEX at 1000 ml/ha; ALLIANCE at 600 ml/ha
- **Mango:** TRIGGER PRO at 1500 ml/ha; WOKOVIT at 1875-2500 gm/ha
- **Cumin:** SANCHAR at 2500 gm/ha

### Downy Mildew
**Best options:** SANCHAR (Metalaxyl + Mancozeb), DUOGUARD (Cymoxanil + Mancozeb), ALLIANCE
- **Grapes:** DUOGUARD at 1500-2000 gm/ha; SANCHAR at 2500 gm/ha; ALLIANCE at 750 ml/ha
- **Potato:** SANCHAR at 2500 gm/ha; DUOGUARD at 1500 gm/ha; BENDACO at 1750 gm/ha
- **Pearl millet:** SANCHAR at 2500 gm/ha
- **Maize:** BENDACO at 1000 gm/ha; DECIMER at 1 ml/lit
- **Cucumber:** DUOGUARD at 1500 gm/ha

### Blast (Rice/Paddy)

- **ROKO** at 1000 gm/ha

## RESISTANCE MANAGEMENT PRINCIPLES

1. **Rotate fungicide classes:** Alternate between SDHI, DMI, QoI, multi-site, and contact fungicides
2. **Use combinations:** Products with dual mode of action (ALLIANCE, DECIMER, BENDACO) reduce resistance risk
3. **BIOMYCIN (Kasugamycin):** No resistance has developed against Paddy blast — preserve its efficacy by not overusing
4. **SPARSH, DOLBY (Mancozeb-based):** Multi-site action; very low resistance risk; ideal for resistance management
5. **HITONE (Immunity booster):** Use as part of program to reduce total fungicide applications

## FORMULATION GUIDE

| Formulation | Full Name | Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| WP | Wettable Powder | Easy to mix; cost effective |
| SC | Suspension Concentrate | Better leaf coverage; no dust |

| WG/WDG | Water Dispersible Granule | No dust; accurate measurement |
| SL | Soluble Liquid | Fast absorption |

- Mix fungicides with **BIOGREEN** micronutrients for combined disease control and nutrition
