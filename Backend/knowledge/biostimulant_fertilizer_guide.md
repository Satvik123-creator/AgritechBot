### Core Benefits Across All BIOZYME Products
- Primary & Secondary (feeder) root development
- Increases Microbial Activity in soil and nutrient uptake
- Develops tolerance for biotic & abiotic stress
- Improves photosynthesis and the greenness of plants
- Enhances bud stimulation for more fruiting/productive tillers

## SPECIALTY BIOSTIMULANTS

### AMAZE XL — Premium Amino Acid + Seaweed
- **Content:** Seaweed (A. nodosum) 8% + L-Amino acids 20%
- **Unique:** Combination of Korean Amino Acids (microbial fermentation) + Norwegian Seaweed (BILT)
- **Amino acids:** All in free L-Form (natural available form); six types of high purity; Low Chlorine & Na
- **Best use:** During Flowers/Fruit Development stage
- **Benefits:** Improves fruit setting, reduces fruit drop, uniform shape/size/color, improves weight & TSS
- **Dose:** 1-2 ml/lit water; Crops: All fruits & vegetables

### REJOICE WG — First WG Formulation Biostimulant
- **Content:** Seaweed extract 8% + Hydrolyzed proteins 5% + Polysaccharides 25%
- **Unique:** First Wettable Granule (WG) formulation in India; FFS (Flowering and Fruiting Stimulation) technology
- **Benefits:** More flowers, reduces flower drop, improves fruit setting, avoids early abortion of bunches
- **Dose:** 1-2 gm/lit water or 200 gm/acre; All crops

## MOBILIZER — Mycorrhizal Biofertilizer

### What It Does
MOBILIZER contains over 90% Arbuscular Mycorrhiza fungus spores using Sol-Gel technology:
- Extends root absorption area by **more than 700 times**
- Functions as root extension to access water & nutrients beyond the Root Zone
- Hyphae absorbs and actively delivers nutrients directly to roots
- Symbiosis increases effective water utilization
- Improves tolerance to stress and resistance to drought

### How It Works
Spores are harvested from fungal culture, made into Gel, diluted with bioavailable nutrients, and coated on Bentonite for better absorption and durability.

## BIOSTIMULANT SCIENCE

### Why Seaweed Works
Ascophyllum nodosum contains:
- **Alginic acid / Alginate:** Improves soil water retention; chelates micronutrients
- **Cytokinins:** Promote cell division; delay leaf senescence
- **Auxins:** Root development and elongation
- **Betaines:** Osmoprotectants; drought and salt stress tolerance
- **Mannitol:** Carbon source for soil microorganisms
- **Vitamins & Minerals:** Macro and micro nutrients

### Why Amino Acids Work
L-Amino acids in free form are directly available to plants:
- Building blocks for protein synthesis
- Chelating agents for micronutrients
- Precursors for plant hormones (Auxins, Ethylene)
- Osmoprotectants under stress conditions
- Energy source to improve root growth
